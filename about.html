About Us

Bringing FinTech Experience Together With the Next Generation of FinTech Companies

FintiQ was founded to provide specialized consultancy services for rapidly growing startups and scaled companies in the financial technologies sector.

While the FinTech industry is growing at a breathtaking pace, the qualified human resources required for this growth are unfortunately not accessible at the same speed. However, there are highly experienced professionals who know the dynamics of the industry and have successfully executed numerous solutions in the market, still actively pursuing their careers.

FintiQ aims to be a platform that brings together FinTech companies in need of such expertise with these valuable professional capacities.

Founded by leaders who have served in senior executive positions in banking and financial institutions for many years, FintiQ also leverages its Advisory Board, composed of experts who continue their active professional careers, to deliver up-to-date, practical consultancy and guidance services to its clients.

Our mission is to connect companies aiming for growth and sustainable success with professionals who have successfully applied their expertise in the field, and to be their companion on this journey.

We offer end-to-end consultancy services in critical areas such as strategy, organization, technology, business development, and regulation, keeping pace with the industry's growth with our human capital and knowledge.
